Cancellation Policy

  • Canceling is as Fast and easy as booking one. We would love to host you, but our simple cancellation process makes sure you receive a quick confirmation and fast refunds in case your plans change.
  • Without an proper ID card of All Adult Guests, Check-in may be restricted. No Refund in this case.
  • 30 days or more prior to arrival: 5% of the total booking amount will be charged.
  • 29-15 days prior to arrival: 50% of the total booking amount will be charged.
  • 14-07 days prior to arrival: No Refund in this case. Only date changes are possible, Subject to availability.
  • Below 7 days or NO Show: NO cancellation or Date Change possible. NO SHOW means Guest not checked-in within 04:00 pm of C/in date without any information.
  • Cancellation During Stay : NO Changes can be done after check in. Full amount will be charged in this case.
  • If the cancellation happens due to CoViD lockdown or related circumstances, we provide a 1 Year credit of that booking amount from the booking date.
  • We do not charge cancellation fees for bookings canceled within 60 minutes of creating the same booking. Any amount paid will be refunded.
  • For WBTDCL & WBFDC property bookings cancellation will be done according to their policy.
  • For special discounted tariff/special package we don't provide any type of refund. Only Date change is possible deducting 5% of booking amount.
