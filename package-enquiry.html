Mukutmanipur Bishnupur Tour Package

Mukutmanipur Bishnupur Tour Package

Day 1 :
Arrive Bankura (BQA)/Bishnupur (VSU) | Mukutmanipur Sightseeing
  • Arrive at Bankura Station by available trains or buses. Our car will pick you up from bankura station or bus-stand. Then take a ride of 55 kms and reach The Second largest earthen dam of India i.e. Mukutmanipur.
  • Check -in at hotel and get refreshed and enjoy the beauty of Mukutmanipur by staying overnight.
  • After lunch visit Kangsabati dam by car or battery operated eco-friendly vehicles which available. A boat ride at the time of sunset will give you a mesmerizing view with some beautiful clicks on your smartphone. Besides you can explore Pareshnath Shiv-Mandir, Musafirana View point and Deer Park.
Day 2 :
Bishnupur Temple town | Panchmura Terracotta village Sightseeing
  • Have breakfast and proceed for visit of Bishnupur temples and handicrafts. Our car will pickup you from hotel and drive 75 KM to reach Bishnupur through forest roads.
  • Visit Maa Mrinmoyee temple, Jorebangla temple, Shyamrai temple, Gar-darja, Chhinnamsta temple, Jore-shreni temples, Radheshyam temple, Nandalal temple with Dalmadal Canon.
  • Have lunch and visit to Lalbandh followed by Acharya Jogesh Chandra Museum. You can buy world famous ‘Baluchari Silk’ Saree from Bishnupur to get a cheaper and authentic one.
  • On return journey visit Panchmura terracotta village. Buy artifacts directly from artisans. You can visit ‘Tridhara temple’ at Panchmura as an added attraction.
  • Return to Mukutmanipur hotel and stay overnight.
Day 3 :
Checkout from hotel and Drop to Jhargram (JGM) / Bankura (BQA) station
  • This morning after breakfast checkout from hotel and proceed for visit of Jhilimili-Sutan Forest. Jhilimili and Sutan Forest also known as “Baro Mile-er Jungle” is 30 Km away from Mukutmanipur.
  • There are viewpoints scattered throughout the forest from where you will get breathtaking landscapes. Enjoy the raw forest and thrill of hair-pin roads over Sutan lake.
  • Then our car drives to Jhargram via Belpahari and Drop to Jhargram station. In between journey you can visit ‘Jhargram Rajbari’ and a mini zoo at Jhargram. Catch train at Jhargram Station towards Howrah/Kolkata by Ispat Express (3:23 pm) / Ghatsila-Howrah Exp. (3:23 pm) with a good experience.

Or,

  • This morning after breakfast checkout from hotel and proceed for Susunia hill sightseeing via Bikna village. Visit Susunia and have lunch.
  • Then our car drives to Bankura Railway Station. Catch train at Bankura Station towards Howrah/Kolkata by Aranyak Express (3:00 pm) / Ruposi Bangla Exp. (5:30 pm) with a good experience.

Bardi Pahar & Jhilimili
